    What is prohibited?

    It is forbidden to smoke cigarettes and vapes Iqos, glo, as well as disposable cigarettes - POSSIBLE). We do our best to make your stay comfortable and romantic and we want to build relationships with our guests based on mutual respect.

    Can I bring food and drinks with me?

    Yes, you can take it with you or order delivery. We also have our own kitchen and bar, prices for goods are indicated at cost.

    Can I pay for tickets on the spot?

    Unfortunately no. Due to high attendance, it is necessary to reserve seats in advance (preferably 1-2 days in advance). We do not want to lose our money, so booking and paying for tickets takes place directly on our website.

    Do tickets arrive right away?

    Yes, after payment, within 15 minutes, the tickets will be sent to your mail, to which you enter.

    Are there discounts for regular guests?

    Necessarily! We do our best to make you have a good time and want to come back to us again. As a bonus for each next visit there is a 5% discount. And also on a birthday, a birthday man, a 50% discount and a nice gift for us.
